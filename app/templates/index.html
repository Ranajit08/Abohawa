Welcome to ABOHAWA

Your go-to weather app for accurate and up-to-date forecasts. Whether you're planning your day or just curious about the weather, ABOHAWA has you covered with real-time data and a user-friendly interface.

Simply enter your location in the search bar to get started and stay informed about the weather conditions in your area.

ABOUT

ABOHAWA is a simple and efficient weather app built with Flask. It provides users with accurate and up-to-date weather forecasts for any location around the world.
